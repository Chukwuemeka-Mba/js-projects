Jamie Adenuga

Grime MC

Since the release of Grime MC, JME has not released anymore albums however he has collaborated numerous times with other Grime Artists in the years of 2020 and 2021. - Featuring alongside Skepta, Jammer, Shorty and Frisco for “Red Card”. - Collaborated with Shorty, Frisco and Capo Lee for Capo’s album “Norf Face”. - Featured in the Female Allstar’s EP “What You Call Disss” - Featured in a song with SBK and Shorty called “Be Careful”
